Frequently ask question

What Can I find on this channel?

 
 
 Music for YouTube, Twitch, Podcasts, Commercials etc.

 

Can I use your music on YouTube/Twitch?

 
 

Yes, all the music that we promote is free to use on YouTube/Twitch with ONE condition -> Give credit to the artist

 

Can I use your music on Facebook, Instagram, or any other social media?

 
 

It depends on the license of the song. If the song is under a Creative Commons (CC-BY) or a Royalty Free license, then you can use it there with ONE condition -> Give credit to the artist

 

Can I use your music in a Commercial, Film, Podcast, or Videogame?

 
 

It depends on the license of the song. If the song is under a Creative Commons (CC-BY) or a Royalty Free license, then you can use it there with ONE condition -> Give credit to the artist

 

How can I know the license of a song?

 
 

We put the license of each song in every video’s description.

 

Where is the video’s description?

 
 

Below each video’s title, there is a word called “Show More”. If you click there, you can see the description of the video.

 

How can I download a song?

 
 

You can download it by your own way or just follow the link in the description.

 

Some Important Rules:

 
 

– You MUST include the full credits in your video/project description.

– You can NOT claim the music/track as your own product.

– You can NOT sell the music/track anywhere.

– You can NOT use the music/track as background music for your own musical work without the artist’s consent.

– You can NOT use the music/track without giving any credits in the video description.

– You can NOT remove or add parts from/to the credits.

– You MUST contact the artist if you wish to use the music on any kind of project outside of YouTube.

– If you don’t follow these policies, you can get a copyright claim/strike.

– If you need more information about using music, please get in touch with the artist.

– This channel is not the owner of the music/tracks.

 

How can I give credit to the artist?

 
 

In your video’s description:

  1. Add the name of the song.

  2. Add the link of our video

  3. Add the link of the artist.
